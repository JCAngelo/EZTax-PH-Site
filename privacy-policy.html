Privacy Statement

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

EZTax ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our website and mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our tax automation and compliance services.

a. Personal and Registration Data

  • Name (individual or business)
  • Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN)
  • Registered address and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Business details (line of business, registration type)

b. Financial and Transaction Data

  • Sales invoices and purchase receipts
  • Vendor and customer details
  • Transaction amounts and tax-related information
  • Bookkeeping entries generated by the system

c. Tax and Filing Data

  • Applicable BIR forms
  • Computed tax liabilities
  • Filed tax returns and supporting documents

d. Technical Data

  • Device information
  • Log data (e.g., access time, usage patterns)
  • Cookies and analytics data (for website experience improvement)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Automating bookkeeping and generating journal entries
  • Computing tax liabilities based on Philippine tax rules
  • Preparing and filing tax returns
  • Providing analytics and financial insights
  • Improving system performance, AI models, and user experience
  • Communicating updates, reminders, and support

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your information only under the following circumstances:

  • With government authorities (e.g., BIR) when filing tax returns on your behalf
  • With trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, payment providers), subject to strict confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data.

These include:

  • Encryption of sensitive information (such as TIN and financial records)
  • Secure databases and access controls
  • Regular system monitoring and updates

Your data is stored securely and only retained as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal requirements.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion or restriction of processing
  • Withdraw consent (subject to legal and contractual limitations)

To exercise your rights, you may contact us using the details below.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As required for tax compliance and legal obligations
  • As necessary for dispute resolution and enforcement of agreements

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve functionality

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may be limited.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
